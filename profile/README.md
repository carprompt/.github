AI-powered car discovery.

Just describe what you need in your own words and

The Problem

Choosing a car online today is surprisingly hard.

You get:

tons of technical specs you don’t really understand or care about
dozens of reviews and comparison pages

But nobody wakes up thinking: "I need 280 horsepower and 310 Nm of torque"
People usually think: "I want something reliable for winter, fun enough to drive, and not too expensive"

So we built CarPrompt.

What it does

CarPrompt turns a simple description into real car recommendations.

You write a prompt - we understand your intent.
You get recommendations that actually make sense.

That’s it.
